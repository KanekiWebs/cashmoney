ABOUT US

Astra is a gaming and entertainment team that houses talent across major esports titles such as Fortnite and Valorant. Astra was started in February 2021, and since then, we’ve focused on keeping our creators to a high standard and providing a platform for underrated players to grow and develop. Our mission is to become one of the most successful esports companies in the world. We want to provide all of the resources necessary to our members to be successful in the gaming industry. Our management team is devoting the time, effort, and passion required to completely develop the Astra brand. We at Astra believe that our community is unique, and we work hard to improve it for our community and the gaming community in general. Astra strives to expand into new communities, esports titles, and overall esports excellence.
